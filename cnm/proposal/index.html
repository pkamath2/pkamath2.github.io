Companion Webpage for

Generative Modelling and Evaluation of Audio Textures

 
 
 
This is a companion webpage for my research proposal at the Department of Communications and New Media
 
 
 
This page outlines the preliminary work conducted to check if Transformer architectures could work with raw audio samples to autoregressively predict the next sample in the time step.
 
 
 
A 8 layer Transformer was trained upon the Brass and Reed instruments from the NSynth dataset for pitches MIDI 64 (~330 Hz) to 76 (~660 Hz). The conditioning parameters were pitch, amplitude and instrument id.
