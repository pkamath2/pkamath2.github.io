About Me!

Hi there! My name is Purnima Kamath, and I welcome you to my website! I am a software engineer by profession and currently leading a team of inspiring technologists at Credit Suisse. I am passionate about technology and learning new things, and guess that's what inspired me to build a professional women's network in Singapore for Women Who Code! I also love painting in oils and at this website you will find pictures of my art work (or atleast the ones I thought I can show the world! :) )

Please connect with me, tell me what you think. Drop me a line or head over to the next Women Who Code event to catchup!
